# AlphaBoard Features

## 📊 Comprehensive Market Tracking
- **Multi-Asset Support**: Track 60+ assets across Crypto, TradFi (Indices), Commodities, and Forex all in a unified dashboard.
- **Real-Time Data Feeds**: Integration with Binance (for crypto) and Yahoo Finance (for traditional markets) for accurate pricing.
- **Customizable Watchlist**: Pin up to 5 assets to your primary dashboard with live status updates.

## 🤖 Institutional-Grade AI Signal Engine
- **GPT-4o Integration**: Employs OpenAI's most advanced model with `temperature: 0` to ensure highly deterministic and logical trading advice.
- **Context-Aware Analysis**: Processes Multi-Timeframe (MTF) indicators (RSI, MACD, SMAs, EMAs, Bollinger Bands) alongside real-time funding rates and live news sentiment.
- **Advanced Technical Mapping**: Automatically extracts and maps 3 critical Support/Resistance levels.
- **Safe Entry Zones**: Determines 1-3 highly probable entry zones to prevent FOMO and ensure optimal risk-to-reward ratios.
- **Capital Preservation First**: Intelligently outputs `HOLD` signals when market conditions are overly volatile or unfavorable, heavily discouraging forced trades.

## 📓 Advanced Trade Journaling & Vision AI
- **Smart Logging**: Record trades with precision metrics (Leverage, Margin Mode, Entry/Exit).
- **Vision-Assisted Entry**: Upload screenshots of open positions (from Binance or Bybit) and have the GPT-4o Vision API automatically extract and log the trade details.
- **Post-Mortem Learning**: The system captures and analyzes closed trades to derive actionable lessons, directly feeding them back into the core AI Engine to improve future signal generation.

## ⚙️ Derivatives & Fundamentals
- **Perpetual Futures Context**: Tracks Open Interest and Funding Rates for crypto assets to gauge institutional positioning.
- **Global Liquidity Metrics**: Real-time integration of BTC/ETH Dominance, Market Capitalization, and FDV.
- **Cashflow & Revenue**: On-chain fee and revenue tracking for major protocols.
